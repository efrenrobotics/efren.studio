This is the beginning of something. I've been meaning to start writing for a while now, and I figured the best way to do it is to just start.

## Why write?

Writing is thinking made visible. When I try to explain an idea in words, I discover gaps in my understanding that I never noticed before. It's humbling and clarifying in equal measure.

I want to write about:

- **Software engineering** — not just how to build things, but *why* certain approaches feel right
- **Design** — the intersection of aesthetics and usability
- **Craft** — what it means to care about the details

## What to expect

Short, honest posts. No SEO-optimized filler. No "10 tips to become a 10x developer." Just observations from someone who's early in their career and paying attention.

> The best time to start was yesterday. The second best time is now.

Let's see where this goes.
